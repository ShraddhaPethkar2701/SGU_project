About SGU

Established in 2017, Sanjay Ghodawat University (SGU) is located in Atigre, Maharashtra. SGU is known for its innovation-driven approach, top-notch infrastructure, and a focus on practical learning and research. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines.

Courses Offered

  • Diploma and Engineering Programs-BTech | MTech | PhD
  • Business & Management-BBA | MBA
  • Science & Reasearch-B.sc | M.Sc
  • Pharmacy & Health Sciences-D.Pharm | B.Pharm | M.Pharm
  • Commerce and Law-B.Com | BBA LLB
  • Short-term Certificate Courses
  • Industry-Oriented Training Programs
